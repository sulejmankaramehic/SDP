About us
We are a group of students, that found it difficult to find tutors during our time studying. So what we decided to? Create a system where students can easily find tutor for the subject/task they need. Since the system is built by students wee knew what wee needed to focus on an that is simplicity and accessability of the system. So enjoy this online tutoring platform and we hope you find what are looking for here? And remember please contact us for any feedback since this is the first version and many more are to come!
